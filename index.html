jQuery Crystal Game

Enter your player names (or click on Cancel to accept the defaults); then take turns collecting (clicking on) crystals to add them to your collection. Each crystal has a random point value from 1 point to 12 points (which is fixed for the duration of the game). Your goal is to collect crystals in order to accumulate points up to, but not exceeding, the point goal, which is a random number from 19 to 120 points (also fixed for the duration of the game).

The first player to hit the goal wins the game. On each turn, you also have the option to pass (by clicking on the Pass button); if both players pass in succession, the player whose points are closer to the goal wins. If during the course of the game either player overshoots the goal, the other player wins.

Clicking on the Settings button allows you to set the random point goal ranges and the random crystal point ranges, which take effect on the next game.

The first player’s turn for the first game is chosen at random; the first player’s turn then alternates in subsequent games. After a game is complete, click on New Game to begin a new game; or optionally click on Reset All to reset everything, including player names and player scores. Good luck! :)
